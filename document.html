Unit 3 _ Manifesto








This unit was about putting my manifesto into words. And then, publishing and enacting it. After creating a responsive website of my topic for manifesto–10 considerations for designers when working on: Multicultural Project and Sociopolitial Engagement–the method I reached out was to print as a memo pad to be installed in designer's physical workspace, and designing a mockup for website's mobile version to solve the website's limited function only able on desktop.







It was a very interesting project, for which I could explore various methods more than any other previous units. Writing manifesto, proofreading and commenting, coding, using mockup software, printing and binding; all these process I've came through were helpful and refreshing. Also, for this project I had to think a lot more about words and language–which I think is a very critical thing to consider as designer. The most difficult thing other than technical limits I faced while coding was actually deciding what to list as manifesto and how to not lose the meaning as much as I can–even translating English to Korean, my first language, was a time-consuming part. Experience from this unit are so valuable and my future projects will definitely be influenced and benefit a lot from it.
